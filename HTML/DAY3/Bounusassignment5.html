Marine Drive, also known as the Queen's Necklace, is a 3.6-kilometre-long boulevard in South Mumbai. This iconic stretch offers breathtaking views of the Arabian Sea, especially during sunset. Whether you're walking along the promenade or capturing the skyline at night, Marine Drive is a must-visit spot in Mumbai.
